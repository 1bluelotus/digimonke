Micaela

Digital Artist

Creating at the intersection of light, color, and feeling — exploring the spaces between what is seen and what is felt. Each piece begins as an impulse, a fragment of mood or memory, and grows outward until it finds its own shape.

Working primarily in digital media, with a deep love for color theory, atmospheric perspective, and the quiet drama of everyday moments. Inspired by golden-hour light, the texture of old photographs, and the way music moves through the body.

Currently open for commissions and collaborations.
